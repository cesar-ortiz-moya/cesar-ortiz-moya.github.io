About

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics focusing on the space economy. My job market research studies how orbital infrastructure and debris dynamics interact with terrestrial economic growth using integrated assessment models. I am on the job market for the 2025–26 cycle.

Affiliations: University of Málaga; Visiting Research Scholar (2025), University of Colorado Boulder.

Research

Job Market Paper

DISE: A Dynamic Integrated Space–Economy Model for Orbital Debris Mitigation Policy Evaluation

This paper develops a dynamic integrated assessment model combining a neoclassical growth framework with a physical model of orbital debris to evaluate mitigation policies and their macroeconomic effects.
